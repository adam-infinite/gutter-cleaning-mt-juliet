When to Repair vs. Replace Your Gutters

Not all gutter problems require complete replacement. In many cases, targeted repairs are the most cost-effective solution:

Good Candidates for Repair:

  • • Isolated leaks at seams
  • • Loose or missing hangers
  • • Minor holes or cracks
  • • Single damaged sections
  • • Gutters less than 15 years old

May Need Replacement:

  • • Extensive rust or corrosion
  • • Multiple sagging sections
  • • Frequent, recurring leaks
  • • Gutters older than 20 years
  • • Significant storm damage
